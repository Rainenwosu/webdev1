Plantain is a staple in West African cooking and can be made in different varieties the best one is when it is fried.

Section 2

fried plantains can be eaten with eggs, as an accompaniment to rice, it can be eaten on its own. it is very versatile and delicious and has been known to start many fights over the last piece.

Section 3

Another way to enjoy plantains are by grilling them. these can also be eaten on its own but it a thousand times better when you eat it with some spicy sauce.
