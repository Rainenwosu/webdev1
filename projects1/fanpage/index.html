Section 1

Plantain is a staple in West African cooking and can be made in different varieties but the best way to eat it is when it is fried.

Section 2

Fried Plantains can be eaten with eggs, as an accompaniment to rice, it can be eaten on its own. It is very versatile and delicious and has known to start many fights over the last piece.

Section 3

Another way to enjoy plantains are by grilling them, these can aslo be eaten on it own but it is a thousand times better when you eat it with some spicy sauce.
